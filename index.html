Access information

Paths: All the paths are negotiable by wheelchair; although there are differing gradients around the park.

Entrances: There is a small pedestrian entrance at the top of Avenue Hill which has a step and so is not accessible for wheelchairs, the other entrances are accessible for wheelchairs.

Events

Litter Pick

18th July 2020 at 11am

Litter Pick At Potternewton Park, some equipment provided (12 adult and 5 children’s litter pickers) bring your own thick gardening gloves and if you have a litter pick please bring one. We meet at the Bowling Green entrance.

We will work in socially distancing teams of six with family groups working together and by all means wear masks if you want. Special bags will be provided and collected, at the Bowling Green together with borrowed equipment at the end of the session.
